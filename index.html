About Us

LeMejor Delicatessen is a Spanish based deli in Waterford city. LeMejor meaning the best. LeMejor's aim is to make every customer satisfied with every food they purchase. We opened in 2012 and have now been open for 11 years. We offer a wide range of products. We offer different varieties of food such as Sandwiches, Wraps, Subs and pastries.
